## Overview

At Teta, we strive to provide the best AI-powered development experience. We understand that sometimes a service may not meet your expectations, which is why we offer a fair and transparent refund policy.

## Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

### Eligible for Refund

You may request a refund if:

1. **Technical Issues**: You experienced significant technical problems that prevented you from using the platform as intended
2. **Service Disruption**: Major service outages or interruptions that affected your work
3. **Billing Errors**: You were charged incorrectly or multiple times
4. **Feature Discrepancies**: Advertised features were unavailable or non-functional

### Usage Limitations

Due to the high computational costs of AI services, refunds are only available if:

- **Less than 5% of your plan's tokens have been used**, OR
- **Less than 500,000 tokens have been consumed** (whichever is lower)

**Example**:
- Pro plan (6M tokens): Refund available if used < 300,000 tokens (5%)
- Pro+ plan (12M tokens): Refund available if used < 500,000 tokens (capped)
- Max plan (50M tokens): Refund available if used < 500,000 tokens (capped)

## Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will **not** be issued in the following cases:

‚ùå Change of mind after extensive usage
‚ùå Usage exceeding the 5% or 500k token threshold
‚ùå Violation of our Terms of Service or Fair Usage Policy

## Why This Policy?

AI services incur substantial costs:

- **Computational Resources**: Each AI interaction requires expensive GPU/TPU processing
- **Model Access**: Access to premium models (Claude, Gemini, etc.) has direct costs
- **Infrastructure**: Real-time preview environments, cloud resources, and data processing
- **Operational Costs**: These expenses are incurred immediately upon usage

Once tokens are consumed, the associated costs cannot be recovered. Our policy ensures fairness while protecting the sustainability of the service.

## How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund, follow these steps:

### 1. Check Your Usage

Before requesting a refund:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Pricing/Subscription**
2. Review your token consumption
3. Verify you're within the eligibility threshold (< 5% or < 500k tokens)

### 2. Gather Evidence

Prepare documentation of the issue:
- Screenshots of errors or problems
- Dates and times of service disruptions
- Description of technical issues encountered
- Any relevant error messages or logs

### 3. Submit Your Request

Contact our support team:

üìß **Email**: support@teta.so

**Include in your email**:
- Your account email address
- Detailed description of the issue
- Evidence (screenshots, error messages, etc.)

### 4. Review Process

Our team will:
1. **Review your request** within 2-3 business days
2. **Verify eligibility** based on usage and issue details
3. **Request additional information** if needed
4. **Process refund** if approved (5-7 business days)

## Refund Processing

### Approved Refunds

If your refund is approved:
- **Full refund** to the original payment method
- Processing time: 5-10 business days
- Access to paid features will be revoked
- Unused tokens will be forfeited
- You may downgrade to the Free (BYOK) plan

## Alternative Options

Before requesting a refund, consider:

### 1. Downgrade Your Plan

If you're not using enough AI interactions:
- Downgrade to a lower tier
- Takes effect at next billing cycle
- No refund needed for current cycle

### 2. Contact Support First

Many issues can be resolved:
- Technical problems can often be fixed
- Usage optimization tips can reduce token consumption
- Feature guidance can improve your experience

### 3. Use BYOK (Bring Your Own Keys)

If you're unsatisfied with paid plans:
- Switch to the Free (BYOK) plan
- Use your own API keys
- Full platform access (except preview environment)

## Billing Cycle & Cancellations

### Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel anytime:
- Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
- Click **"Manage"** to access the billing portal
- Cancel subscription
- Access continues until end of billing period
- No refund for remaining days

### Automatic Renewal

- Subscriptions auto-renew monthly
- Cancel before renewal date to avoid charges
- Token limits reset on renewal date

## Fair Usage Policy

To prevent abuse:
- **Multiple refund requests** may result in account review
- **Repeated cancellations and resubscriptions** may be flagged
- **Suspected abuse** may lead to account suspension
- We reserve the right to refuse service to users who violate this policy

## Special Circumstances

### Technical Failures

If Teta experiences major technical issues:
- We may proactively issue credits or refunds
- Affected users will be notified via email
- Compensation will be proportional to the disruption

### Force Majeure

No refunds for issues outside our control:
- Third-party API outages (Claude, Gemini, etc.)
- Internet service provider issues
- Natural disasters or acts of God
- Government restrictions or regulations
